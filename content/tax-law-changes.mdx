## Staying Ahead of Change

Tax laws are updated regularly at the federal and sometimes state level. Understanding what changed — and what did not — helps you plan income, deductions, and timing.

## What to Watch Each Year

- Bracket and standard deduction adjustments for inflation  
- Credit and deduction phaseouts and limits  
- Retirement contribution caps  
- Business and self-employment rules  

## Why It Matters

A rule that applied last year may not apply the same way this year. Planning with outdated assumptions can mean surprises at filing time or missed planning opportunities mid-year.
