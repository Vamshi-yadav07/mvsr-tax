## Make the Most of Legitimate Write-Offs

Small business owners have many opportunities to reduce taxable income — if expenses are ordinary, necessary, and well documented.

## Home Office

If you use part of your home exclusively and regularly for business, you may qualify for a home office deduction using the simplified or regular method.

## Vehicle and Travel

Business miles, parking, and travel that is primarily for business can often be deducted. Keep a mileage log and separate personal trips.

## Equipment and Software

Computers, tools, and subscriptions used for the business may be deductible or eligible for Section 179 or bonus depreciation, depending on the asset and tax year rules.

## Professional Fees

Legal, accounting, and consulting fees tied to your business are generally deductible.

## Retirement Plans

Contributions to SEP IRAs, SIMPLE IRAs, or solo 401(k) plans can lower taxable income while building savings.
